Privacy Policy

Introduction

Photos & Files Recovery helps users recover deleted photos, videos, audios, and documents. The app also provides tools to back up and restore contacts locally on the device only, remove duplicate files, clean cached WhatsApp media, and delete junk files.

All recovery and cleaning operations are performed directly on the user's device. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data or recovered files with third parties.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that you have full control over your files while using the app.

Permissions and Their Uses

To provide reliable file recovery and cleaning features, Photos & Files Recovery requires access to certain parts of your device. Below is a clear explanation of why each permission is needed and how it is used:

Contacts Permission:

These permissions allow the app to back up and restore your contacts directly on your device. For example, if you accidentally delete a contact, the app can restore it from the local backup. Your contact list is never uploaded to any server and remains only on your phone.

Storage Permission (for Android 10 and below):

The app needs access to your storage to scan for deleted photos, videos, audios, and documents. With this permission, the app can check your memory for files that can be recovered, and also save recovered files back to your device. Without this access, file recovery would not be possible.

Full Access Permission (for Android 11 and above):

Starting from Android 11, access to files is more restricted. This permission is required so the app can fully access your files and folders to:

  • Recover deleted photos, videos, audios, and documents.
  • Detect and remove duplicate files that waste space.
  • Clean up cached WhatsApp media to free storage.
  • Delete unnecessary junk files.

This permission is used only for recovery and cleaning tasks, and your data is never collected or shared outside your device.

Important Notes:

  • Before requesting any permission, the app displays an explanatory message to inform the user why the permission is needed, ensuring full transparency.
  • Permissions are requested only when a feature requiring them is used. No permission is activated automatically or in the background.

Data Collection

Data Collected by the App:

Photos & Files Recovery performs all file recovery, backup, and cleaning operations locally on your device. The app does not collect any personal information from users, nor does it upload or share recovered files, contacts, or media with any external servers. All actions are performed on the device to ensure your privacy.

Data Collected by External Services:

Some external services used by the app may automatically collect non-personal information, such as device type, operating system, app usage patterns, crash reports, and approximate location.

The external services used include:

  • AdMob – for displaying ads
  • Firebase Analytics – for analyzing app usage
  • Firebase Crashlytics – for detecting crashes
  • Google Play Billing – to process in-app purchases

For more details on what data is collected and how it is used, please review the privacy policies of each of the services listed above.

Data Security

At Photos & Files Recovery, we are committed to protecting users' data by implementing technical and organizational measures designed to secure your information and prevent unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

  • All recovery, backup, and cleaning operations are performed locally on your device without uploading any files or personal data to external servers, minimizing the risk of data leakage.
  • We do not collect or store any sensitive personal information on our servers.
  • The external services we use, such as AdMob, Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, and Google Play Billing, adhere to high security standards and strict privacy policies to ensure user data protection.

However, no electronic system can guarantee 100% data security. Therefore, we advise you to take necessary precautions when sharing or storing your personal data.
